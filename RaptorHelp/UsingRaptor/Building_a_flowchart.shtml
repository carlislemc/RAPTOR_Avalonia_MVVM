Building a flowchart

To build a flowchart, left click on a symbol in the Symbol Window.  Move the mouse to the place in the flowchart where the symbol belongs. You may need to move the cursor slightly to find an insertion point. When the cursor is at an insertion point, the cursor will change to .  When the left mouse button is clicked at an insertion point, the selected symbol is added to the existing flowchart at the specified location.

 

If symbols are incorrectly placed, select Undo, Delete, or Cut from the Edit or Right Mouse Button menu. Symbols may also be copied and pasted in another area of a flowchart.

 

Once a symbol has been correctly placed, double click the symbol to edit its contents.
